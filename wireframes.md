## Design Notes

### Color Palette Options:

**Option 1: Warm & Cozy**
- Background: Soft cream (#FFF9F0)
- Accent: Coral (#FF6B6B)
- Text: Deep brown (#2D2A26)

**Option 2: Modern Dark**
- Background: Deep navy (#0F0F1A)
- Cards: Soft white (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: Electric purple (#8B5CF6)

**Option 3: Playful Pastel**
- Background: Soft lavender (#F5F3FF)
- Accent: Mint green (#10B981)
- Secondary: Peach (#FBBF24)

### Typography:
- Headlines: SF Pro Display / Plus Jakarta Sans / Satoshi
- Body: Inter / DM Sans

### Key Interactions:
- Carousel auto-scrolls slowly, pauses on hover
- Cards lift on hover with subtle shadow
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Cat preview animates on hover
